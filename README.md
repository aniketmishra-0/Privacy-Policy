# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: February 2, 2026_

**Where is my Vehicle** ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

---

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Location Data:** We access your location only when you use tracking features. Background location is collected only if you allow it.
- **Account Info:** Email, name (if you sign in), and profile photo (if you use Google sign-in).
- **Device Info:** Device model, OS version, and unique device ID.

## 2. How We Use Your Data
- To provide vehicle tracking and show your location on the map
- To send notifications and geofence alerts
- To improve our app and services
- We do **not** sell your data to third parties

## 3. Data Security
- Your data is stored securely and encrypted on trusted servers (Firebase/Google Cloud)
- Only authorized users can access your data

## 4. Data Sharing
- With fleet managers/brokers (if you are part of a fleet)
- With legal authorities if required
- With service providers (Google Maps, Razorpay) for app features

## 5. Your Rights
- You can view, delete, or request deletion of your data at any time
- You can disable location tracking in your device settings
- Account deletion removes all your personal data
